Innovation

Innovation in video games

  • Growing up I fondly remember acquiring a new game and it containing some groundbreaking fresh mechanic that I had to learn and that was apart of the fun
  • Unfortunately the industry has reached a point where publishers dictate and influence the developers and has created an era of stagnation

How can I contribute?

  • Voice my disatisfaction with the industry
  • Implement a mechanic into a game to demonstrate the improvements showcase what players are being starved of

How can you contribute?

  • If you are truly unhappy with a game that you were once anticipating, speak up
  • The complacency of the industry is in part due to the players have become numb to poorly made, lackluster products because its what we've come to expect from studios

Hopefull outcome

  • The complacency is no longer a problem and the rug is pulled from under studios feet if they expect the community to just accept what we get
  • We start to see NEW mechanics and creativity is bred back into the industry stoking the fire and creating a spark of creativity and innovation for the next generation
